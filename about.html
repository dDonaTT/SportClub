About KB Peja

Welcome to the official website of KB Peja, the leading basketball team in our region. Established in 1948, we have a rich history of success and sportsmanship.

History

KB Peja was founded in 1948 and has since grown to become one of the most successful basketball teams in our region. Our journey is marked by numerous championships and a commitment to excellence.
